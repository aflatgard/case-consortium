Welcome to the Case Consortium at Columbia University.

Our ever-growing case collection includes "teaching" cases for the fields of journalism, public policy, public health, and other disciplines. Nearly all the cases are multimedia and based on original research; a few are written from secondary sources. All cases are offered free of charge.
